Encouraging career growth and development is key to creating unique Red Hatter experiences that drive engagement, improve retention, break down organizational silos, and highlight versatile career paths. Red Hat offers numerous ways to help you see beyond today and find new ways to map your career to internal opportunities. Explore the many ways Red Hat Internal Gigs can connect you to people and projects that align to your skill sets, aspirations, and development interests. Internal gigs are designed to provide on-the-job skill development by matching associates with opportunities, including short term initiatives. This can include stretch assignments, job shadowing and special projects. Internal gigs provide opportunities for associates to maximize their talents and passion while developing new skills, perspectives, and experiences.
